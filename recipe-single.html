Simple sirloin steak

Ingredients

Method

STEP 1
Heat the olive oil in a large heavy-based pan. Add the onions, carrots and thyme leaves, then gently fry for 15 mins.

STEP 2
Stir in 1.4l chicken stock, bring to a boil, cover, then simmer for 10 mins.

STEP 3
Add the chicken, peas and Greek yogurt, then simmer for 10 mins.

STEP 4
Mix the Greek yogurt, garlic and lemon juice together. Ladle the soup into bowls, swirl in the garlic yogurt, then serve.

STEP 4
If you want to use a slow cooker, gently fry the onions, carrots and thyme leaves for 15 mins, then tip them into the slow cooker with 1 litre stock. If you're using a chicken carcass, add it now. Cover and cook for 2-3 hours on High until the veg is tender. If you used a carcass, remove it now, shredding any remaining chicken from the bones.
